Design Principles Assignment 1 on Redesigning an App Logo

Redesigned Signal App Logo.
Design Rationale: Signal App Icon :
The S in the textbox represent Signal. I decided to incorporate the letter S into the textbox so that it will better reflect their app name. For the colour scheme, I used blue as the background because it symbolise stability and trust. It is to represent that Signal is a secure and encrypted messaging app. I added gradient to the background to make it more colourful and appealing. I use Grey for textbox and White for the letter S because I feel that it kind of look nice on Blue gradient background and will catch the user's attention to the icon. It is to represent that Signal is a secure and encrypted messaging app

Created with Adobe Photoshop
