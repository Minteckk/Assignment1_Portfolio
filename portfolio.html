DP Assignment 3 on Designing a Infographics

Created a Infographics for Signal
Created with Adobe Photoshop
Design Rationale:
I decided to use light blue because it is a lively colour. Light blue symbolise trustworthiness and reliability which is suitable and important for a messaging app. I included the ratings score of Signal on both Google Play and iOS for the user to know whatever the app has a good rating or not. I also included some good reviews from the users to promote the app I included the section on “why use Signal”? to promote the app and provides the users with two reasons on why they should consider using signal. I decided to incorporate the feedback that I received and added rectangle boxes to organise and break the different contents apart for better readability and make full use of the space in the infographics.
